Another technical challenge that we faced was to find a free resource to connect the facebook ads data to data studio.How the Technical Challenges were SolvedTo solve the technical challenges we went through the documentation of the mixpanel api to get a understanding of how the things work. Based on that we built the pipeline to connect the mixpanel data to big query.
